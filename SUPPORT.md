# Support

Thank you for using FFishAsia.

## What FFishAsia does
FFishAsia is an AR field guide for Asian animals and plants. It lets you browse curated 3D models, download them on demand, and view saved models offline after download.

## Common notes
- AR viewing requires camera permission.
- Model downloads require an internet connection.
- Downloaded models can still be viewed later without re-downloading.
