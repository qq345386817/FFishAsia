Little Nature for Mac is a desktop field guide for browsing CC0-based 3D biological model records from Asian animals and plants. It gives you a larger workspace for exploring the catalog, reviewing model details, and managing saved model content.

Use it for natural history reference, education, model selection, or simply browsing the shapes and taxonomy of flowers, insects, amphibians, marine life, and other organisms.

Key features:
- Desktop catalog: browse curated 3D animal and plant model records on Mac
- Category browsing: filter by All / Animals / Plants / Special
- Quick search: find models by Japanese, English, Chinese, or scientific name
- On-demand downloads: download models only when needed to reduce storage use
- Offline access: review saved model content after it has been downloaded
- Download management: check saved models and clear local content
- Model details: view face count, vertex count, file size, category, and source links

Model resources are curated from public sources and used under CC0 license terms for browsing and local reference. Little Nature does not provide user accounts or upload personal data.
