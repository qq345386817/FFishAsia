# Privacy Policy

FFishAsia respects your privacy.

## What the app accesses
- Camera: used only to place and preview AR models in your surroundings.
- Local storage: used to save downloaded model files on your device.

## What the app does not do
- No account registration or login
- No collection of personal profile data
- No upload of your photos, camera feed, or downloaded model files to our servers
- No third-party advertising SDKs

## Model downloads
When you choose to download a model, the file is downloaded to your device so you can open it later, including offline for already downloaded content.

## Data retention
Downloaded model files remain on your device until you delete them inside the app or remove the app.
